Noise Pollution Videos

Below are videos I've collected of noise pollution in the form of a deep, oscillating sound emanating from building straps used at the construction site by Drysdale Road.

On some days the noise is not much of a problem, however on days where this is a bit of wind these straps can oscillate and cause a great deal of noise in local residential areas.

Whilst construction noise is to be expected, when this noise continues into the night and is very audible within flats, this can affect the wellbeing and health of local residents.

Due to the low and deep nature of the sound, the videos below are best listened to using headphones. As the sound tends to come and go, it's worth listening to a video in its entirety.
